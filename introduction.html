Understanding the Minimax Algorithm

The Minimax algorithm is a cornerstone of artificial intelligence (AI) in game theory, designed to make optimal decisions in turn-based games like Tic Tac Toe, Chess, and Checkers. It operates by evaluating possible game moves and choosing the best one to minimize potential losses while maximizing gains.

How Does It Work?

Minimax systematically explores all possible moves, assigning numerical values based on their potential impact on the game outcome. The AI then selects the move with the best possible result, ensuring an optimal strategy against an opponent.
